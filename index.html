Exterior House Painting Tips


If you’re looking to give your house a fresh new look, exterior house painting is a great way to do it! But before you get started, there are a few things you should keep in mind. Here are some tips to help make the process go as smoothly as possible.

Painting tips: Choose the right paint.

There are a lot of different types of paint on the market, so it’s important to choose one that is right for your needs. If you’re not sure what kind of paint to use, ask a professional at your local hardware store for advice.

There are two basic types of paint: oil-based and water-based. Oil-based paint is more durable and resistant to scratches and stains, but it is also more difficult to work with. Water-based paint is easier to use and clean up, but it doesn’t last as long.

When choosing a paint, consider the following factors:

  • The type of surface you’re painting (wood, metal, plaster, etc.)
  • The amount of traffic in the area (high traffic areas will need more durable paint)
  • The climate (paint that can withstand extreme weather conditions is best for outdoor use)
  • Your personal preference (some people prefer the look of oil-based paint, while others prefer water-based paint)

Painting tips: Prepare the surface.


Once you’ve chosen your paint, make sure to properly prepare the surface before beginning to paint. This means filling any cracks or holes and sanding down any rough areas. By taking the time to do this, you’ll ensure that your paint job looks its best.

Once you’ve prepared the surface, it’s time to start painting! Begin by using a primer if you’re painting over a dark color or if you’re painting a light color on a dark surface. This will help ensure that your paint job is even and looks its best. Once you’ve applied the primer, you can begin painting with your chosen color. Be sure to use smooth, even strokes and take your time. If you rush, the paint job will likely look sloppy.


When you’re finished painting, be sure to clean up any messes promptly. This includes spilled paint, drips, and smudges. All of these can ruin the look of your newly painted surface. So, be sure to clean them up as soon as possible.

With these tips, you should be able to successfully paint any surface in your home. Just remember to take your time, use a primer, and clean up any messes immediately. With a little bit of effort, you can achieve beautiful results that you’ll be proud of.

Painting tips: Use a primer.

Once you have prepared the surface and chosen the right primer, it is time to start painting. When painting the exterior of your home, it is important to start at the top and work your way down. This will help to prevent any drips or runs from happening.

Paint in sections.


When painting the exterior of your house, it’s best to work in sections. This will make the job go faster and help you avoid missed spots. Begin at the top of your house and work your way down. Be sure to also paint the trim and gutters to give your home a finished look.
